# Consulting24 — Backlink & Guest-Post Outreach Kit

Goal: earn editorial backlinks from established crypto/fintech/legal sites to lift
consulting24.co indexation and rankings. Authority is the #1 ceiling for a young domain.

**Golden rules**
- Relevance > volume. A link from a crypto/fintech/legal site beats 50 random ones.
- Earned/editorial links only. Never buy "do-follow packages." Paid/sponsored placements
  must use `rel="sponsored"` (Google treats undisclosed paid links as a violation).
- Vary anchor text: mostly brand ("Consulting24") or natural phrases ("Panama crypto setup
  guide"), not exact-match keywords every time.
- One or two contextual links per article, deep into the page where it genuinely helps.

---

## 1. Target types (in priority order)

1. **Crypto/fintech news & blogs** that accept guest contributions or expert columns.
2. **Legal-tech / company-formation / offshore blogs** (high topical overlap).
3. **Startup & founder communities** (Indie Hackers, founder newsletters, niche Substacks).
4. **HARO / journalist-request platforms** — Mardo as a quotable expert source.
5. **Podcasts / YouTube interviews** in the crypto-founder space (show notes link back).
6. **Reputable directories / profiles**: Crunchbase, Clutch, GoodFirms, LinkedIn company page,
   Estonian e-Business Register, niche crypto-service directories.
