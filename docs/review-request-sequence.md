# Client Review-Request Sequence (Trustpilot + Google Business Profile)

Goal: turn 500+ real, satisfied clients into third-party trust corroboration — the single
largest untapped E-E-A-T signal Consulting24 owns. Trustpilot and Google Business Profile
appear in the brand SERP, raise YMYL trust, and increase AI entity-confidence.

## Compliance (read first — non-negotiable)
- **Neutral, non-incentivized ask.** Never offer a discount, gift, or anything of value for
  a review (FTC + Trustpilot/Google policy). No gating ("only review if you're happy").
- **Ask ALL completed clients**, not just the ones you think are happy — selective
  solicitation is a policy violation and skews the corpus.
- **No fake or employee reviews. No templated copy the reviewer pastes.** Invite honest,
  first-hand experience in the client's own words.
- **Timing:** send after the engagement is genuinely complete (company live / licence
  granted), when the value is fresh — not mid-delivery.
- Set up the Trustpilot business profile and claim the Google Business Profile first, so the
  links resolve.

## Sequence (2 touches, ~7 days apart, then stop)

### Touch 1 — email, day 0 (engagement complete)
> Subject: How did your setup with Consulting24 go?
>
> Hi {first_name},
>
> Now that your {jurisdiction} setup is complete, we'd value your honest feedback — it helps
> other founders judge whether we're the right fit, and it helps us improve.
>
> If you have two minutes, a short review would mean a lot:
> • Trustpilot: {TRUSTPILOT_REVIEW_URL}
> • Google: {GBP_REVIEW_URL}
>
> Whatever your experience, we'd genuinely like to hear it.
>
> Thank you,
> Mardo Soo — Founder & CEO, Consulting24

### Touch 2 — WhatsApp or email, day ~7 (only if no review left, once)
> Hi {first_name} — just a gentle follow-up on my note. If you have a moment, an honest
> review of your Consulting24 experience would really help other founders: {TRUSTPILOT_REVIEW_URL}.
> No worries at all if you'd rather not. Thanks again.

Then stop — no third chase.

## Placeholders to fill
- `{TRUSTPILOT_REVIEW_URL}` — from the claimed Trustpilot business profile
- `{GBP_REVIEW_URL}` — Google Business Profile "write a review" short link
- `{first_name}`, `{jurisdiction}` — merge fields from CRM/delivery list

## Operating notes
- Run against the full completed-client list in small weekly batches (steady, natural
  velocity beats a one-day spike).
- Respond publicly and professionally to every review, positive or critical — response rate
  is itself a trust signal.
- Track: reviews collected, average rating, response rate. Feed the count/rating into the
  brand SERP and (once genuine) the site's trust signals.
