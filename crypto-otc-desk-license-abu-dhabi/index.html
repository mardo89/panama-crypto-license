What Is a Crypto OTC Desk License in Abu Dhabi?

A crypto OTC desk license in Abu Dhabi, granted by the ADGM FSRA, authorises a firm to engage in over-the-counter trading of digital assets. Unlike exchange-based trading, OTC desks execute large orders directly between buyers and sellers, often with negotiated prices and without order book visibility. This licence falls under the Financial Services Permission (FSP) category for 'Operating a Multilateral Trading Facility' or 'Dealing in Investments as Principal or Agent', depending on the business model.

The ADGM framework is based on international standards, including those of the Financial Action Task Force (FATF). It requires strong AML/KYC procedures, capital adequacy, and fit-and-proper assessments of key personnel. As of 2026, the ADGM is recognised as a 'green' jurisdiction by the FATF, meaning it has a strong regulatory environment.

Who Needs This License?

The crypto OTC desk license in Abu Dhabi is suitable for:

  • Institutional trading desks that handle large block trades for hedge funds, asset managers, and high-net-worth individuals.
  • Market makers providing liquidity in digital assets.
  • Brokerage firms that want to offer OTC services alongside exchange trading.
  • Family offices and proprietary trading firms dealing in cryptocurrencies.

If your business model involves executing trades of more than, say, $100,000 per transaction, an OTC desk structure is often more efficient than using a retail exchange. The Abu Dhabi license gives you credibility and access to the region's growing crypto ecosystem, including banking relationships with local and international banks.

License Type & Regulator

The regulator is the Financial Services Regulatory Authority (FSRA) of the Abu Dhabi Global Market (ADGM). The license type is a Financial Services Permission (FSP) with specific scope for digital asset activities. Depending on your exact activities, you may need one or more of the following 'regulated activities':

  • Dealing in Investments as Principal or Agent - for buying/selling digital assets.
  • Operating a Multilateral Trading Facility (MTF) - if you operate a platform that brings together multiple buyers and sellers.
  • Arranging Deals in Investments - for introducing counterparties.
  • Advising on Investments - if you provide investment advice related to digital assets.

The ADGM FSRA is known for its rigorous but fair approach. It requires a detailed business plan, risk management policies, and a local presence (a physical office in the ADGM).

Capital Requirement

The ADGM FSRA sets minimum capital requirements based on the type of activities. For a crypto OTC desk license, the typical capital requirement is:

  • Category 1 (Dealing as Principal): AED 10 million (approx. USD 2.7 million) or higher, depending on risk.
  • Category 2 (Dealing as Agent): AED 2 million (approx. USD 545,000) or higher.
  • Category 3 (Arranging): AED 500,000 (approx. USD 136,000).

These figures are subject to FSRA discretion and may be adjusted based on your business plan and risk profile. The capital must be maintained as liquid assets in a bank account in the ADGM. In contrast, Panama has no minimum capital requirement for its crypto license, making it a more accessible option for startups.

Tax Treatment in Abu Dhabi

Abu Dhabi offers a highly attractive tax environment for crypto businesses:

  • 0% corporate income tax for most activities within the ADGM, as long as you do not have a branch in the UAE mainland.
  • No VAT on digital asset transactions (as of 2026, the UAE VAT law exempts certain financial services).
  • No capital gains tax on trading profits.
  • Withholding tax: Generally 0% on dividends and interest paid to non-residents.

However, you must comply with the OECD's Common Reporting Standard (CRS) and may need to register for VAT if you provide other taxable services. Panama also offers territorial taxation (only income sourced in Panama is taxed), but its corporate tax rate is a flat 25% on Panamanian-source income. For many crypto OTC desks, Panama's tax regime can be more favourable if structured correctly.

Allowed Activities Under the License

With a crypto OTC desk license in Abu Dhabi, you are permitted to:

  • Execute OTC trades in digital assets (cryptocurrencies, tokens, stablecoins).
  • Provide liquidity to institutional clients.
  • Offer custody services for digital assets (if separately licensed).
  • Facilitate token swaps and block trades.
  • Provide market-making services.

The license does not automatically allow you to operate a retail exchange, offer derivatives, or issue tokens. Each of those activities requires separate authorisation. The FSRA may also impose conditions, such as limits on the types of assets you can trade or the maximum transaction size.

Compliance & Trust

Compliance is at the core of the ADGM regime. Licensees must implement:

  • AML/CFT policies aligned with FATF recommendations.
  • KYC procedures for all counterparties.
  • Transaction monitoring systems to detect suspicious activity.
  • Regular reporting to the FSRA (e.g., quarterly financial statements, annual audit).
  • Appointment of a Money Laundering Reporting Officer (MLRO).

Common Mistakes When Applying

Avoid these pitfalls:

  • Underestimating capital requirements: Ensure you have sufficient liquid funds before applying.
  • Incomplete documentation: The FSRA expects a comprehensive business plan and policies. Missing details can delay the process.
  • Ignoring fit-and-proper requirements: All directors and shareholders must pass background checks. Any adverse history can be a deal-breaker.
  • Choosing the wrong license category: Some firms apply for an MTF license when they only need a Dealing license, leading to unnecessary costs.

Frequently asked questions

What is a crypto OTC desk license in Abu Dhabi?

It is a Financial Services Permission (FSP) from the ADGM FSRA that allows a firm to execute over-the-counter trades of digital assets directly between counterparties, typically in large volumes.

How much does an Abu Dhabi crypto OTC desk license cost?

What is the minimum capital requirement for an OTC desk license in Abu Dhabi?

It varies: AED 10 million (USD 2.7M) for dealing as principal, AED 2 million (USD 545K) for dealing as agent, and AED 500,000 (USD 136K) for arranging. The FSRA may adjust based on risk.

How long does it take to get a crypto OTC desk license in Abu Dhabi?
