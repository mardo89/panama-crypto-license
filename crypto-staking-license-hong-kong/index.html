What is a Crypto Staking License in Hong Kong?

A crypto staking license Hong Kong is not a standalone license but is covered under the Virtual Asset Service Provider (VASP) license issued by the SFC. As of 2026, any platform offering staking services to Hong Kong residents must be licensed. Staking involves locking up cryptocurrencies to support network operations and earn rewards. The SFC treats staking as a regulated activity when the platform has custody or control over client assets.

The regulatory framework is based on the Anti-Money Laundering and Counter-Terrorist Financing Ordinance (AMLO) and the Securities and Futures Ordinance (SFO). Staking of tokens that are considered securities may also require a Type 1 (dealing in securities) or Type 9 (asset management) license. However, most staking services for major tokens like Ethereum or Solana fall under the VASP regime.

Consulting24 advises clients on the precise classification of their staking model. We recommend a thorough legal review to determine if a VASP license suffices or if additional licenses are needed. For example, a platform that stakes both utility tokens and security tokens may need dual licensing. The SFC has published guidelines on staking, requiring clear disclosure of risks, lock-up periods, and slashing conditions. Non-compliance can lead to enforcement actions, including fines or license revocation.

Who Needs a Crypto Staking License in Hong Kong?

Any entity that provides staking services to Hong Kong retail or professional investors likely needs a VASP license. This includes:

  • Cryptocurrency exchanges offering staking pools
  • Staking-as-a-service platforms
  • Wallet providers that stake on behalf of users
  • DeFi protocols that are centrally managed and solicit Hong Kong users

If you are a non-Hong Kong entity targeting Hong Kong clients, you must also be licensed. The SFC has extraterritorial reach. For instance, a Singapore-based staking platform that markets to Hong Kong residents must apply for a VASP license. License Type and Regulator

The regulator is the Securities and Futures Commission (SFC). The license type is a Virtual Asset Service Provider (VASP) license under the AMLO. Additionally, if staking involves security tokens, a Type 1 or Type 9 license under the SFO may be required. The SFC has issued guidelines on staking, requiring clear disclosure of risks, terms, and custody arrangements.

As of 2026, the SFC has a two-tier system: for professional investors only (higher net worth) and for retail. Retail-facing staking services are subject to stricter rules, including a mandatory risk assessment and cooling-off periods. The SFC also requires that licensed firms have at least two responsible officers (ROs) who are fit and proper. Capital Requirement

Hong Kong does not have a statutory minimum capital requirement for VASP licenses. However, the SFC expects firms to maintain adequate financial resources. In practice, the SFC may require a minimum paid-up capital of HKD 5,000,000 (approx. USD 640,000) for retail-facing platforms. For professional-only platforms, a lower amount may be acceptable. Additionally, firms must maintain liquid capital of at least HKD 2,000,000.

Tax Treatment

Hong Kong operates a territorial tax system. Only profits sourced in Hong Kong are taxable. Staking rewards are generally considered taxable income. The profits tax rate is 16.5% for corporations. There is no capital gains tax. However, the Inland Revenue Department (IRD) has not issued specific guidance on staking, so tax treatment is determined on a case-by-case basis.

Consulting24 recommends engaging a Hong Kong tax advisor. For staking rewards, the IRD may treat them as trading income if the activity is frequent and organized. Alternatively, if held as long-term investments, they may be capital in nature and not taxable. Panama offers territorial taxation with no tax on foreign-sourced income, including staking rewards. For example, a Panama-licensed entity earning staking rewards from non-Panama sources pays 0% tax. This can be a significant advantage for global operations.

Allowed Activities

Under a VASP license, the following staking-related activities are allowed:

  • Operating a staking pool for clients
  • Delegating client assets to validators
  • Distributing staking rewards to clients
  • Providing staking as part of exchange services

Prohibited activities include: offering unlicensed securities, using client assets for proprietary staking without consent, and misleading marketing. The SFC requires clear disclosure of lock-up periods, slashing risks, and reward calculations. Consulting24 helps draft compliant terms of service. Step-by-Step Application Process

The process for a crypto staking license Hong Kong involves several stages:

  1. Pre-application: Engage a consultant (like Consulting24) to assess your business model and determine the appropriate license type.
  2. Company incorporation: Set up a Hong Kong company with a physical office and local directors.
  3. Compliance documentation: Prepare AML/CFT policies, risk assessment, business plan, and financial projections.
  4. Submit application: File Form VASP-1 with the SFC along with supporting documents and fee.
  5. Review and interview: The SFC may request additional information and conduct interviews with key personnel.
  6. Approval: If successful, the SFC issues a VASP license with conditions (e.g., for professional investors only).
  7. Post-licensing: Ongoing reporting, audits, and compliance.

Frequently asked questions

What is the difference between a VASP license and a Type 1 license for staking?

A VASP license under AMLO covers virtual asset services like staking of non-security tokens. A Type 1 license under SFO is needed if the staked tokens are securities. Many platforms need both. Consulting24 advises on classification.

Can a foreign company apply for a Hong Kong staking license without a local office?

No. The SFC requires a licensed entity to have a physical office in Hong Kong and at least two local directors. Consulting24 helps with company incorporation and office setup.

How long does the SFC take to process a VASP application?

Typically 6 to 12 months. Delays occur if documents are incomplete. Consulting24 prepares a thorough application to minimize back-and-forth.

Is staking of Ethereum considered a regulated activity in Hong Kong?

Yes, if the platform has custody of client ETH and stakes it on their behalf. The SFC treats this as a virtual asset service requiring a VASP license.

What are the ongoing compliance obligations after obtaining the license?

Firms must submit annual audits, appoint a compliance officer, conduct AML training, and report suspicious transactions. The SFC conducts periodic inspections.

Can I use a Hong Kong license to serve clients in mainland China?

No. Mainland China prohibits crypto trading and staking. The Hong Kong license is only for services to Hong Kong residents and permitted jurisdictions.

What is the cost of renewing a Hong Kong VASP license annually?

The SFC annual fee is HKD 5,000. But total compliance costs including legal, audit, and compliance officer salary typically range from HKD 100,000 to 200,000.

Does Panama allow staking services under its crypto license?

Yes. Panama's license covers all virtual asset services, including staking, with no restrictions. The flat fee is EUR 6,000 and no minimum capital. Consulting24 delivers directly.

What happens if I offer staking without a license in Hong Kong?

It is a criminal offense. The SFC can impose fines up to HKD 5,000,000 and imprisonment. Unlicensed platforms may also face asset freezing and customer lawsuits.

Can I stake security tokens under a VASP license only?

No. Staking security tokens requires a Type 1 or Type 9 license in addition to the VASP license. Consulting24 helps determine the correct license combination.

How does Hong Kong's tax treatment of staking rewards compare to Panama?

Hong Kong taxes staking rewards as income at 16.5% if sourced in Hong Kong. Panama taxes only Panama-sourced income; foreign staking rewards are tax-free.
