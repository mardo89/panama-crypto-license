What Is a Crypto OTC Desk License?

A crypto OTC desk license is a regulatory authorisation that permits a company to conduct over-the-counter trading of crypto assets. Unlike exchange trading, OTC desks handle large blocks of trades with negotiated prices, often for institutional clients or high-net-worth individuals. The license typically covers activities such as:

  • Buying and selling crypto assets as principal or agent
  • Providing custody of client funds and crypto assets
  • Executing trades on behalf of clients (brokerage)
  • Offering price quotes and market-making in certain jurisdictions

In the EU, under MiCA, an OTC desk license falls under the Crypto Asset Service Provider (CASP) regime. In Panama, a Panama company with a crypto license can operate an OTC desk with a flat cost of EUR 6,000. Consulting24 advises and coordinates licensing in all major crypto-friendly jurisdictions. The license ensures that your OTC desk complies with AML/KYC regulations, protects client assets, and meets capital adequacy standards. For example, a typical OTC desk handling trades of EUR 500,000 or more per transaction would need to demonstrate strong risk management and reporting procedures.

License Type and Regulator

The regulatory body and license type depend on the jurisdiction. In the EU, the European Securities and Markets Authority (ESMA) coordinates national regulators under MiCA. Each EU member state designates a competent authority: for example, the Bank of Lithuania or the Estonian Financial Supervision Authority (FSA). Under MiCA, a CASP license covers OTC desk activities. In Panama, the regulator is the Superintendencia de Bancos de Panamá (SBP) for crypto-related activities, though the licensing process is managed through the Public Registry and the Ministry of Commerce. Capital Requirement

Capital requirements are jurisdiction-specific. Under MiCA, CASPs must hold minimum capital based on the services they offer:

  • EUR 50,000 for custody and transfer services
  • EUR 125,000 for exchange and trading services
  • EUR 150,000 for full-service OTC desks including market-making

In Panama, there is no statutory minimum capital for a crypto OTC desk license, but the company must demonstrate sufficient financial resources to operate. Typically, a paid-up capital of USD 10,000 is recommended. Consulting24 advises on the appropriate capital structure for your business. For example, if you plan to offer custody alongside OTC trading, you may need to meet the higher capital tier under MiCA. Tax Treatment

Tax treatment of OTC desk income varies. In Panama, companies are taxed on income sourced in Panama at a rate of 25%, but offshore income (from clients outside Panama) may be exempt. In Lithuania, corporate income tax is 15% (or 5% for small companies), and crypto trading profits are taxable. Estonia offers a unique 0% tax on retained profits, with 20% upon distribution. Under MiCA, no specific crypto tax regime exists, so general corporate tax rules apply. Consulting24 recommends consulting a tax advisor for your specific situation. Step-by-Step Application Process

The process for obtaining a crypto OTC desk license typically involves these steps:

  2. Company incorporation - Register a legal entity (e.g., in Panama or Lithuania). This includes drafting articles of association, appointing directors, and registering with the commercial registry.
  3. Document preparation - Business plan, AML/KYC policies, risk management framework, and financial projections. These must be tailored to your specific OTC desk operations.

  5. Regulatory review - The regulator assesses compliance and may request additional information. This stage can involve interviews with key personnel.
  6. License issuance - Once approved, you receive your license and can begin operations. You may need to post a security bond or maintain a minimum capital balance.

Frequently asked questions

What is a crypto OTC desk license?

A crypto OTC desk license is a regulatory authorisation that allows a company to conduct over-the-counter trading of crypto assets, including executing large trades, providing custody, and offering brokerage services. It ensures compliance with AML/KYC regulations and capital requirements.

How much does a crypto OTC desk license cost in Panama?

Panama offers a flat fee of EUR 6,000 for company setup and license. This is one of the most cost-effective options globally. Additional costs may include legal fees for document preparation, but Consulting24 includes these in the flat fee.

What are the capital requirements for an OTC desk license under MiCA?

Under MiCA, capital requirements are EUR 50,000 for custody, EUR 125,000 for exchange/trading, and EUR 150,000 for full-service OTC desks including market-making. These must be maintained as own funds.

Can I operate an OTC desk without a license?

Operating without a license is risky and often illegal. In the EU, unlicensed CASP activity can result in fines up to EUR 5 million or 5% of annual turnover. In Panama, unlicensed crypto businesses may face closure and reputational damage.

How long does it take to get a crypto OTC desk license?

Timelines vary: Panama takes 4-6 weeks, Lithuania and Estonia take 3-6 months, and other EU jurisdictions may take 4-8 months. The timeline depends on the completeness of your application and the regulator’s workload.

What documents are needed for an OTC desk license application?

Typical documents include a business plan, AML/KYC policies, risk management framework, financial projections, background checks on directors, and proof of capital. Consulting24 provides templates and guidance.

Is Panama a good jurisdiction for a crypto OTC desk?

Yes, Panama offers low cost, fast setup, and no statutory capital. However, it may not be ideal if your target market is the EU, as Panama is not an EU member. It is excellent for serving clients in Latin America, Asia, and the US.

What are the ongoing compliance obligations for an OTC desk license?

Ongoing obligations include AML/CFT reporting, annual audits, transaction monitoring, capital maintenance, and periodic regulatory filings. Consulting24 offers compliance support packages to help you meet these requirements.

Can Consulting24 help with banking for my OTC desk?

Yes, we can introduce you to compliant banking partners in Panama, Lithuania, and Estonia. We have relationships with banks that accept crypto clients subject to enhanced due diligence.

What is the difference between a CASP license and an OTC desk license?

A CASP license under MiCA covers a broad range of crypto services, including OTC trading. An OTC desk license is essentially a CASP license that specifically authorises OTC activities. The terms are often used interchangeably.
